Importance of blood donation

The blood transfusion process is one of the operations that contribute to saving lives. It is given in such cases: When complications occur for pregnant women, such as bleeding before, during or after childbirth. For patients during surgeries such as heart operations, blood vessels, organ transplant surgery, and others. for those with blood diseases. for those injured in accidents. cancer patients

The steps taken to ensure the safety of the blood being transferred to the patient:

Passing the medical examination after answering the questions related to the health history and fulfilling the general conditions for blood donation.
Conducting accurate analyzes and examinations on each blood unit to know the blood type and ensure that it is free of infectious diseases, such as (AIDS, hepatitis B and C, syphilis, and malaria).
Perform compatibility tests between transfused blood units and patients' blood; This is to ensure the safety and effectiveness of blood.
Use of filters for leukocytes.
